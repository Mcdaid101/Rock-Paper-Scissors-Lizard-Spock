Rock Paper Spock!

How to play


This is a game similar to Rock, Paper, Scissors but with a lizard and star trek's spock added in..


These are the rules:

Scissors cuts Paper

Paper covers Rock

Rock crushes Lizard

Lizard poisons Spock

Spock smashes Scissors

Scissors decapitates Lizard

Lizard eats Paper

Paper disproves Spock

Spock vaporizes Rock

Rock crushes Scissors


Click the icon to choose your variable, the computer will then pick it's own variable and the winner will be revealed. The first to win 5 rounds is the victor!
